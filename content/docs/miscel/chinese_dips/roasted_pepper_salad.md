# Roasted Pepper Salad
![Roasted Pepper Salad](https://cookage-recipe-src.s3.amazonaws.com/user-data/fisher/miscel/841690312537_.pic_hd.jpg)


**Serves: 1-6**

- 6-8 Roasted peppers
- 2/3 C Olive Oil
- 1/4 C red wine vinegar
- 2 T EACH Chopped Parsley + basil
- Salt of Pepper
